Economic growth

Astek has shown strong growth since 2020, with revenue rising from €350 million to nearly €700 million in 2024. The company aims to reach €1 billion by 2027.

Sectoral diversification

The group operates in five main sectors: aerospace and defense, energy and healthcare, finance, retail, and telecommunications. This diversification supports its economic stability.

International Presence

Nearly 55% of Astek’s revenue is generated outside France, mainly in Europe and the Americas. The company aims for balanced global expansion.

Job Creation

Astek currently employs around 8,400 people and aims to reach 14,000 by 2027. This hiring momentum is driven by both organic growth and acquisitions.
