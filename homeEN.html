Welcome to Astek

Accelerate your digital transformation with our expertise.

Founded in 1988, Astek is a French digital services company that experienced rapid growth in 2015 through external expansion and the opening of international offices. Now present in 23 countries, the company provides services in digital, data, telecoms and intelligent industry, serving sectors such as transportation, finance and government. Astek has succeeded in placing people at the heart of its corporate vision...

With a strong increase in revenue, from 200 million euros in 2018 to over 600 million in 2023, Astek is expanding rapidly. Since its creation, the company has made numerous acquisitions such as Eurelys and Catep, and opened several international branches, including in Spain (Madrid and Barcelona in 2015) and Australia (Sydney). In 2023, Astek employs more than 10,000 people worldwide...

Astek’s headquarters are located in Boulogne-Billancourt, on the outskirts of Paris, at the heart of France’s economic and technological center. This strategic location places the company among the key players in the digital industry, institutions, competitiveness clusters and decision-making centers. By choosing to establish itself in this dynamic area, Astek demonstrates its commitment to staying close to its partners, clients and talents, while remaining connected to major innovation trends...
