Astek Presentation

Astek is a French digital services company founded in 1988. Since 2015, the revival of an external growth strategy, a rebound in revenue, the opening of new international offices, and awards for innovative projects have marked a new turning point in the group’s history.

What is Astek today?

The 3 business areas:

Digital & Data

New methodologies and technologies enable organizations to rapidly achieve a successful digital transformation of their work environment. Current data analytics offers companies opportunities to innovate, control their production and supply chains, and optimize processes and industrial performance.

Telecom & Infrastructure

One of the most essential aspects of digital transformation is the crucial role of internal data and digital communications within organizations.

Intelligent Industry

Digital technologies are driving a major transformation of operational and organizational models. The implementation of “smart factories” offers an extraordinary field of innovation, progress, and growth across industries.
